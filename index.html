FAQ

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about our platform and digital maturity. If you have any other questions, please feel free to contact us.

What is digital maturity?

Digital maturity is the degree by which an organisation has moved to operating digitally, as opposed to operating face-to-face or on paper. It is not just about technology, but also about culture, processes, people and outcomes.
